# ONBOARDING — FrontierCRM in 5 minutes

FrontierCRM is a multi-tenant CRM. Your team gets an isolated workspace (a **tenant**) where you manage contacts, track deals through pipelines, sync email, assign tasks, and log activities.

## Login or sign up

1. Open https://app.frontiercrm.com
2. **Sign up** with email + password, or use **Google OAuth** to auto-create an account.
3. Your first signup creates your organization (tenant). You're the owner.
4. **Invite teammates** from Settings → Team. They join your tenant.

## Core features

| Feature | Use it to... |
|---------|-------------|
| Dashboard | See pipeline value, win rate, active deals, tasks due this week. |
| Contacts | Store people + companies. Link deals, notes, and email. |
| Pipeline | Drag deals through stages (Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed). |
| Activities | Timeline of calls, meetings, emails, and deal stage changes. |
| Email | Connect Gmail to sync inbound/outbound email to contact records. |
| Tasks | Todo items with priority, assignee, and due date. |
| Notes | Free-form notes attached to contacts, deals, or accounts. |
| Settings | Team management, profile, integrations. |

## Quickstart

```
1. Sign up → you land on the dashboard
2. Click Contacts → Add Contact (fill in name, email, company)
3. Click Pipeline → drag that contact into a deal
4. Click the deal → add a note, attach a file, log an activity
5. Connect Gmail from Settings → Integrations
6. Invite your team from Settings → Team
```
