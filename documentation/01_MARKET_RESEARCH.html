    Market Segmentation Analysis

    SMB Segment (10-200 employees) — PRIMARY TARGET

    Size: ~30,000 companies (US) — growing at 8% CAGR

    What this segment wants: - Affordable, predictable pricing under $30/user/mo - Fast setup (< 1 day to value) - Beautiful, intuitive UI — no training required - Email + calendar sync out of box - Pipeline management that works immediately - Basic reporting — 5-10 key dashboards - Mobile access with parity to web

    What this segment hates: - Setup fees, onboarding costs - Forced annual contracts - Feature gates — being asked to upgrade for basic reporting - Complex interfaces with features they don't need - Poor customer support - Data migration difficulty

    Top competitors for this segment: HubSpot, Pipedrive, Freshsales, Zoho, Copper

    Mid-Market Segment (200-1,000 employees) — SECONDARY TARGET

    Size: ~10,000 companies (US)

    What this segment wants: - Sophisticated reporting with custom dashboards - AI-powered forecasting and lead scoring - Advanced automation and workflow engine - Deep integrations (Slack, Teams, accounting, ERP) - Role-based access and team management - Integration with existing tech stack - Good support (phone preferred)

    Top competitors for this segment: HubSpot (Enterprise), Salesforce, Dynamics 365, Zoho

    Market Gaps & Opportunities

    Gap Current State Opportunity for FrontierCRM
    Pricing transparency Every major CRM has confusing pricing tiers, feature gates, and hidden add-ons Single all-in per-user price — no tiers, no gates
    AI as core, not add-on Einstein, Copilot, Breeze, Freddy — all upsells at $50-200+/mo extra AI at every tier, included in base price
    Modern UX Salesforce is '90s enterprise; HubSpot is decent but dated; Zoho is cluttered Build with 2025 design principles — fast, clean, responsive
    Mobile parity Every CRM mobile app is a pale shadow of its web version Mobile-first architecture — full functionality offline
    Unified communications Calls, emails, chats, social in separate interfaces across every CRM Unified smart inbox with AI triage
    Painless switching Switching CRMs takes weeks and costs thousands in consulting One-click import from any major CRM
    Built for SMB (not enterprise-down) Salesforce/Dynamics built for enterprise then stripped down; HubSpot moved upmarket Build for SMB from the ground up — simple when simple is needed, powerful when needed
